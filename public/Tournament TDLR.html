üìã General Rules

  • Algerian community tournament is a 1v1 osu!standard tournament with open rank range.
  • Only players with a profile flag from Algeria are eligible ‚Äî including Algerians living abroadThis rule has exceptions .
  • All matches will be played using Head to Head, ScoreV2, and NF (No Fail) enforced.
  • The top 16 players from the qualifiers will advance to the next stage.
  • All participants must join the Discord server.
  • Staff members (other than streamers, commentators, referee and graphic designers) are not allowed to participate.
  • Players must be respectful at all times.

üéØ Qualifiers

  • Players are responsible for scheduling a lobby that fits them
  • Lobbies should be scheduled 24 hours in advance. Exceptions from this rule can be granted after talking to staff.
  • Players play through the qualifier pool once (Played in fixed order NM1 ‚Üí DT2).
  • If a player miss/be late to their lobby, they are allowed to reschedule for a new time, or ask the referee to play the missed maps at the end of the lobby
  • Qualifier round used to determine seeding for the bracket stage.
  • Mappool includes 11 maps:
    • 5 NM, 2 HD, 2 HR, 2 DT
  • 2-minute ready timer, 5-minute grace period
  • Top 16 players advance to Round of 16 (RO16).

üèÜ Bracket Stage

Double Elimination format.

Match Formats by Round:

  • Round of 16 (RO16) : BO9 (Best of 9)
  • Quarterfinals (QF) & Semifinals (SF) : BO11 (Best of 11)
  • Finals (F) & Grandfinals (GF) : BO13 (Best of 13)

Freemod Rules:

  • FM map requires at least one of the following mods: HD, HR, FL, EZ.
  • Mods combination is allowed.
  • EZ has a 1.8x score multiplier.

Tiebreaker:

  • The TB (Tiebreaker) will be played with FM (Freemods) enabled.
  • Mods are optional on TB (Tiebreaker), but NF (NoFail) is always enforced.

‚öôÔ∏è Match Procedures

  • A referee will create the match lobby before the scheduled start time and invite both players.
  • Players are required to arrive on time and be fully prepared. Referees may exercise discretion if a player appears unready.
  • If a player fails to join within 5 minutes of the scheduled match time, they will lose both their roll and bans.
  • If a player fails to join within 10 minutes, they will forfeit the match.
  • Warmups are allowed.

üé≤ Rolling, Picks & Bans

  1. Both players must use the !roll command.
  2. The player with the higher roll chooses either:
    • To Ban Second, and Pick First.
    • To Ban First, and Pick Second.
  3. The loser chooses the remaining option.
  4. Double picking the same mod is allowed, Double banning not allowed

    5. üö´ Ban System Details:

    • Each player gets 2 bans:
    • The first ban occurs at the start of the match (1 per player).
    • The second ban occurs after 4 maps have been played.
    • Ban order follows ABBA format:
    • Example: Player A bans ‚Üí Player B bans ‚Üí (after 4 maps) Player B bans ‚Üí Player A bans

    ‚è∞ Timers

    • Players are given90 seconds to declare a ban. Failure to do so results in losing that ban.
    • Players are given90 seconds to declare a pick. If they fail, a random map will be selected using !roll.
    • Once a map is picked, both players have 90 seconds to ready up. After that, the referee will force start the match.

    üîå Disconnection

    • If a player disconnects within the first 30 seconds of a map, a replay may be granted.
    • Each player is allowed a maximum of 1 replay per match.
    • If a player disconnects later in the map:
      • If clear score proof exists, it may be used to determine the result.
      • If the outcome is unclear, the point goes to the opposing player.
    • Acceptable disconnection causes: hardware failure, system crash, internet loss.
